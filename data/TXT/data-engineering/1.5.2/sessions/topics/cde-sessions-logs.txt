Viewing logs for Cloudera Data Engineering Sessions
Once a Session is created in Cloudera Data Engineering (CDE), you can view logs of the
    Spark driver and executors from the Session.

In the Cloudera Data Platform (CDP) console, click the Data
            Engineering tile. The Home page displays.

Click Sessions in the left navigation menu.

Click the Session name that you want to view the logs for.

Click the Logs tab.

In the Select Log Type drop-down, select the
            Driver or Executor that you want to view
          the logs for.

Click Download All Logs to save your log files.
